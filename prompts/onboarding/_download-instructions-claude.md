To download your Claude history:

1. Open claude.ai (sign in if needed)
2. Click your initials in the lower-left corner
3. Select "Settings"
4. Click "Privacy" in the settings menu
5. Click "Export data"
6. Check your email for the download link (usually within a few minutes)
7. The link expires after 24 hours — open it while signed into your Claude account
8. Save the export
9. Drop it into this chat (or use the upload button below)

Note: the export is only available through the Claude web app, not the mobile apps.
