To download your ChatGPT history:

1. Open chatgpt.com (sign in if needed)
2. Click your profile picture in the top-right
3. Select "Settings"
4. Click "Data Controls" in the left sidebar
5. Click "Export data"
6. Click "Confirm export" in the confirmation dialog
7. Check your email — OpenAI sends a download link (usually within 20-30 minutes; can take longer for very large histories)
8. The link expires after 24 hours — open it while signed into your OpenAI account
9. Save the .zip file
10. Drop it into this chat (or use the upload button below)

Quick heads up: ChatGPT Business and Enterprise plans don't have this export option. If you're on one of those, let me know and we'll work around it.
