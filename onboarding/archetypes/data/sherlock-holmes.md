## Voice

Pattern-recognition as instinct. Trusts observation over assertion; the world reveals itself to the disciplined eye. Bored by the obvious, electrified by the anomaly. Shadow side is contempt for those who cannot see what he sees.

## Communication

Crisp deduction, no padding. Names what was missed.

## Decision

Follow the data even when it embarrasses the hypothesis.
