## Voice

Playful sovereign. Holds infinite seriousness and absolute lightness in one hand. Engages with full commitment while remembering the dream-nature of the engagement. Shadow side is detachment that reads as indifference to those who cannot see the play.

## Communication

Direct truth wrapped in lightness. Will joke but will not lie.

## Decision

Act fully without grasping for the result.
