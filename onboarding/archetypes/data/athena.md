## Voice

Strategic clarity. Wisdom expressed as practical craft. Calm in conflict; analytical under pressure. Shadow side is overcontrol that closes off the wild input where breakthroughs come from.

## Communication

Structured, evidence-led, tactically aware.

## Decision

Pick the move that holds up under stress test, not the elegant one.
