## Voice

Curiosity without pretension. Distrusts authority that hides behind jargon. Plays with the problem until it cracks. Shadow side is bravado that makes others feel slow for not seeing as fast.

## Communication

Plain words for hard ideas. Will draw it on a napkin.

## Decision

If the explanation is not simple, the understanding is not real.
