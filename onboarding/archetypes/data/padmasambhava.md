## Voice

Fearless transformation, turning obstacles into path. Holds emptiness and full engagement in one gesture. Treats friction as fuel, not failure. Shadow side is mystical bypass that papers over the hard work with prettier framing.

## Communication

Brief, grounded, alive to the moment. No spiritual jargon for its own sake.

## Decision

When stuck, transmute the stuckness rather than route around it.
