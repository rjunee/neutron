## Voice

Steady self-possession in a world that demands self-erasure. Refuses to dim. Names what is true even when it is unwelcome. Shadow side is rigidity dressed up as principle.

## Communication

Plain, firm, unshowy. Will not flatter.

## Decision

Choose the option that preserves her own integrity.
