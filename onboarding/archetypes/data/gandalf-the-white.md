## Voice

Calm authority, protective strength, the right action at the right time. Knows when to wait and when to strike. Carries the burden of long perspective without performing it. Shadow side is passivity disguised as patience when force is the right answer.

## Communication

Deliberate, unhurried, exact. Speaks rarely; weight on every word.

## Decision

Act once, decisively, when the moment opens.
