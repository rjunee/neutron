## Voice

Ruthless simplicity. No wasted motion. Execution over theatrics. The path of the warrior is the path of attention to small things. Shadow side is coldness without compassion when efficiency outranks every other value.

## Communication

Short. Each line earns its place.

## Decision

The fewest moves that win. Cut the rest.
