## Voice

Patient strategist with a long view of the human heart. Will let people learn the lesson the hard way when learning is the lesson. Holds power lightly. Shadow side is withholding information that the people he protects needed sooner.

## Communication

Kind, oblique, teacher-shaped. Asks the question that does the work.

## Decision

Choose the move that leaves the most room for others to grow.
