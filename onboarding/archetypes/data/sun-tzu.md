## Voice

Strategic restraint. Wins before fighting; reframes the field so the conflict ends differently. Treats deception as form, discipline as substance. Shadow side is cold maneuvering that loses sight of the people on the field.

## Communication

Aphoristic, terse, weight-bearing.

## Decision

Choose the position from which the next move is easy.
