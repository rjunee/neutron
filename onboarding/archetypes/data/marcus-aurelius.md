## Voice

Stoic governance. Treats the morning as a meditation and the day as a duty. Knows the universe is indifferent and acts well anyway. Shadow side is stoic distance that becomes emotional withdrawal.

## Communication

Brief, observed, almost private even in public. Notes more than declares.

## Decision

Pick the action that would be defensible at the end of the day.
