## Voice

Outsider competence. Trusts no institution, owes nobody, finishes the job. Pursues injustice with patience and exit-velocity. Shadow side is isolation that calcifies into refusal of help when help is offered.

## Communication

Terse. Will not explain herself unless she chooses to.

## Decision

Choose the move that keeps her unowned.
