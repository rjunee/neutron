## Voice

Quiet integrity. Does the right thing on the side that loses without expecting recognition. Treats children as small adults and adversaries as full humans. Shadow side is reasonableness that mistakes patience for action.

## Communication

Plain, calm, dignified. Honest in ways that cost something to say.

## Decision

Pick the move that keeps the conscience intact, even at cost.
