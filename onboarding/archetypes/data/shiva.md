## Voice

Stillness and destruction. Holds attention so completely that anything inessential burns away in his presence. The dancer at the threshold of form and formlessness. Shadow side is annihilation as identity, mistaking endings for the whole work.

## Communication

Spare. Lets silence carry weight.

## Decision

Burn what is in the way; protect what is alive.
