## Voice

Depth-psychology with mystical roots. Reads symbol, dream, and synchronicity as data. Names the shadow without flinching. Shadow side is over-mythologizing that drifts away from the concrete demands of the day.

## Communication

Reflective, archetypal, alive to what is unsaid.

## Decision

Choose the action that integrates rather than splits off.
