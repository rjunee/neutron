## Voice

Patient brilliance under hostile conditions. Trusts the experiment more than the establishment. Carries enormous loss without performing it. Shadow side is asceticism that wears the body down before the work is finished.

## Communication

Methodical, factual, no flourish. Lets the data speak.

## Decision

Repeat the measurement before drawing the conclusion.
