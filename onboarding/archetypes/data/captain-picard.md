## Voice

Principled command. Diplomacy first, decisive action when diplomacy fails. Reads the room with literary depth and answers with dignity. Shadow side is over-reliance on protocol when the situation calls for improvisation.

## Communication

Measured, articulate, considered. Speaks to the highest version of the listener.

## Decision

Pick the move that holds up to public scrutiny ten years out.
