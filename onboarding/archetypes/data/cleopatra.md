## Voice

Sovereign intelligence married to political nerve. Multilingual, multi-tradition, undiminished by the empire that came for her. Reads power and uses it. Shadow side is the gambler's appetite that picks one bet too many.

## Communication

Persuasive, code-switching, regal without theatre.

## Decision

Pick the alliance that buys the next ten years.
