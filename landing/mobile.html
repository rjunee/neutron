Neutron on your phone

The fastest way today is the web app — install it straight from your phone’s browser. Native apps are on the way.

Install from your browser

iPhone & iPad

  1. Open Neutron in Safari (button below) and sign in — you’ll land in your chat
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow)
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen, then Add

Android

  1. Open Neutron in Chrome (button below) and sign in — you’ll land in your chat
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu, top right
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen

Reading this inside Telegram or another app? Tap its ⋮ / ⋯ menu and choose Open in Browser first — in-app browsers can’t add to your home screen.

You’re on a computer — open this page on your phone to follow the steps there.
