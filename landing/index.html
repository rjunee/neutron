Neutron

Self-hosted · local-first · no account

Your agent harness is ready

Neutron is an agent harness you run on your own machine — your files, your keys, your gateway. There's nothing to sign into. Start it from a terminal and connect from the web or the app.

Quickstart first run
~/neutron
$neutron up
harness listening on http://localhost:8787 access token paste-this-into-the-app

Runs on your box

The gateway, your data, and every agent transcript stay on the machine you start it on.

Bring your own keys

Point it at the model provider you already pay for. Nothing routes through a hosted service.

Open source

Read it, fork it, extend it. The harness ships with its source — no black boxes.

Already running? Open the chat surface or wire up the app.
